HISTORY

A Decade of Excellence

Founded in 2014, SiteScaffold began as a small design boutique with a big vision: to create digital experiences that truly matter. What started in a shared workspace has grown into a global agency.

Over the years, we've navigated the shifting landscapes of technology and design, always staying ahead of the curve. Our journey has been defined by the partnerships we've built and the challenges we've overcome.

Today, we are proud to work with industry leaders and innovative startups alike, bringing the same level of passion and expertise to every project, regardless of scale.
